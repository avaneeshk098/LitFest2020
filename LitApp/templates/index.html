Welcome to Meraki 2020!

Literature goes beyond books

About Meraki 2020

Everybody’s first thought when someone says literature is dusty books and even dustier statues. But the world of literature and language encompasses a vibrant and widespread variety of things that go beyond just Shakespeare and Dickens. And this is exactly what Meraki 2020 is all about. So join us for a day filled with events, games and competitions that celebrate literature for what it truly is - diverse, spirited and most of all - FUN.

Timeline of Meraki 2020

Juniors (6 - 8)

  • 8:00am - 8:30am

    Inauguration and Slam Poetry

    Slam poetry is a unique form of performance that combines literature with the spoken word. Participants recite their original work within a given time period, and aim to engage the audience with their body language, articulation, and of course—their poem. Poems are encouraged to be on current social topics and the poet’s interpretation of them.

  • 8:30am - 9:15am

    Myth Storytelling

    If you love myths and folktales, this event is certainly for you! Participants narrate stories from myths, aiming to engage listeners with their method of storytelling.

  • 9:30am - 10:30am

    Crime Whodunit

    Think you are the next Sherlock Holmes? Find out by solving this classic whodunnit case full of puzzles and riddles that will surely make you put your thinking caps on! This will be a fun and exciting online stimulation!

  • 10:30am - 11:15am

    Sci-Fi

    The Back to the Future event is all about bringing your favorite elements of science fiction to life! This can be done in any form; ranging from blueprints to full-scale models. Along with this, we have a debate on a topic related to a theme which is widely discussed in science fiction literature.

  • 11:15am - 12:15pm

    Comics

    Lovers of comics, manga, or webtoons can show off their artistic talent by creating a newspaper-style comic in one hour with a positive message embedded into it.

  • 12:45pm - 1:45pm

    Poetry Live Writing

    Calling all budding poets to harness their creativity. A poem on an unseen and predefined picture and prompt has to be produced within a given time limit. May the best poet win!

  • 2pm - 3pm

    Stand Up Comedy + Closing Ceremony

    Tickle your funny bone and join the stand up comedy event, featuring live virtual comic performances by participants!
